2. **Risk Assessment:** Every trading decision carries a certain level of risk. It is important to assess this risk before making a decision. This includes understanding the potential loss and the likelihood of this loss occurring.

3. **Diversification:** To mitigate the risks associated with trading, we should diversify our portfolio. This means investing in different types of assets, sectors, and geographical locations.

4. **Consistent Evaluation:** The market is dynamic and constantly changing. Therefore, we need to regularly evaluate our trading strategy and make necessary adjustments. This includes reviewing our portfolio performance, assessing market trends, and adjusting our strategy accordingly.

5. **Emotion Control:** Trading decisions should be based on logic and not emotions. We should avoid making impulsive decisions based on short-term market fluctuations. Instead, we should focus on the long-term performance and potential of our investments.

6. **Education and Learning:** The trading market is complex and constantly evolving. Therefore, continuous learning and staying updated with the latest market trends and trading techniques is crucial for our success.

7. **Regulatory Compliance:** Ensure all our trading activities are in compliance with the relevant regulatory bodies. This includes understanding and adhering to the rules and regulations set by these bodies.
