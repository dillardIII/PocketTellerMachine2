In order to maximize our trading success, I propose the following logic strategy:

1. **Market Analysis:** Before making any trading decisions, we should conduct a thorough analysis of the market trends. This includes studying the economic indicators, political events, and other significant factors that could influence the market.

2. **Risk Management:** We should never risk more than a small percentage of our trading capital on a single trade. This will ensure that we can withstand any potential losses and stay in the market for the long term.

3. **Diversification:** We should diversify our portfolio by investing in different asset classes. This will help to spread the risk and increase the potential for returns.

4. **Discipline:** We should always stick to our trading plan and avoid making impulsive decisions based on emotions. This includes setting a stop loss and take profit levels for each trade.

5. **Continuous Learning:** The market is constantly changing, and so should our trading strategies. We should always be open to learning and adapting to new market conditions.

6. **Performance Review:** Regularly reviewing our trading performance will help us identify our strengths and weaknesses. This will enable us to make necessary adjustments to our trading strategy.

7. **Patience:** Trading is not about making quick profits, but about waiting for the right opportunity. We should be patient and avoid overtrading.

8. **Use of Technology:** We should leverage the power of technology to automate our trading processes. This includes using trading software and algorithms to execute trades.

By adhering to these principles, we can increase our chances of achieving consistent trading success.
