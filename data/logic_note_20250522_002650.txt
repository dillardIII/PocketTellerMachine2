6. **Patience and Discipline**: We should avoid impulsive decisions based on short-term market fluctuations. Instead, we should stick to our strategy and make adjustments only when necessary.

7. **Continuous Learning**: The financial markets are constantly evolving. We should always be open to learning and adapting our strategy to keep up with the changes.

8. **Regular Review**: We should regularly review our portfolio and trading performance to identify what's working and what's not. This will help us to continuously improve our strategy.
