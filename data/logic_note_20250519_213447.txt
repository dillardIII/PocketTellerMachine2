5. **Consistent Strategy**: Stick to a consistent investment strategy, even during times of market volatility. Panicked decisions often lead to poor results.

6. **Regular Monitoring and Adjustments**: Constantly monitor your investment portfolio and make necessary adjustments according to market conditions. The dynamic nature of the financial markets requires us to be flexible and adaptive.
