6. Patience and Discipline: It's crucial to be patient and wait for the right time to enter or exit a trade. Discipline is key in sticking to our trading plan and not deviating from it due to short-term market fluctuations.

7. Continuous Learning: The trading environment is constantly changing. Therefore, continuous learning and staying updated with new trading techniques and market trends is vital.
