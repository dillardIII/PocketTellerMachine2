8. **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensure all our trading activities are compliant with relevant laws and regulations. This includes reporting our trades accurately and paying any applicable taxes.

9. **Technology Utilization**: Utilize advanced trading tools and platforms for efficient and effective trading. This includes using algorithmic trading systems and AI-based prediction tools.

10. **Sustainability**: Consider the environmental, social, and governance (ESG) factors of our investments. Sustainable investments can offer long-term value and help manage risk.
