2. **Diversification:** Spreading investments across a wide range of assets will reduce the risk of significant losses. 

3. **Risk Management:** Always consider the risk-to-reward ratio before entering a trade. It is logical to avoid trades where the potential loss is greater than the potential gain.

4. **Market Research:** Stay updated with global and local economic news. This will help us understand market trends and make informed decisions.

5. **Consistent Evaluation:** Regularly review and adjust our trading strategies based on their performance and market changes.

6. **Patience:** Trading is a long-term game. It is logical to wait for the right opportunity rather than rushing into trades.

7. **Emotional Control:** Make decisions based on logic and strategy, not emotions. Emotional trading often leads to poor decisions and losses.

8. **Learning:** The trading landscape is constantly evolving. Continuous learning and adapting is the only logical way to stay ahead.
