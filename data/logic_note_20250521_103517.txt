Subject: Logic Strategy Note 

Dear Team,

1. Information Analysis: Always start by thoroughly analyzing the product or asset we are interested in trading. This will include a study of its past performance, current status, and future projection. Understand and make note of its strengths, weaknesses, market trends, and any external factors influencing it.

2. Consider Market Trends: It would be best if you always analyze the market trends, both short-term and long-term, to make informed decisions. The market's environment could help forecast the direction in which trends will move.

3. Timing: In trading, timing is key. Always know the right moment to enter or exit a trade. This is determined by careful analysis of market trends and price patterns.

4. Risk Management: Always have a risk management strategy in place. Be clear about your risk tolerance and the amount of capital you are willing to risk on each trade. It is advisable not to invest more than 1-2% of your trading capital on a single trade.

5. Diversification: Diversify your investment portfolio. It's safer not to put all your eggs in one basket. By spreading investments across various assets, we can mitigate potential losses.

6. Regular Review: Regularly review and adjust your trading strategy based on profits and losses. This helps in identifying what works best and improving efficiency.

7. Stick to Strategy: Do not let emotions guide your trading decisions. Stick to the plan and strategy, even during tough trade periods, only make logic-based decisions. Emotional responses can often lead to impulsive and costly decisions.

8. Continuous Learning: The market is dynamic and constantly changing. Keep educating yourself on market trends and different trading strategies. This will help us to always stay on top.

Please keep these pointers in mind as we execute our trading endeavors. They should serve as a guide to help us make sound trading decisions.
