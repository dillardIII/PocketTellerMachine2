Dear Team,

I hope this note finds you well. As we navigate the dynamic world of trading, it's crucial to employ a logic-based strategy to ensure our decisions are driven by data and rational analysis rather than emotions or hunches. Here are some key points to consider:

1. **Market Research:** Always base your trading decisions on thorough market research. Understand the market trends, economic indicators, and the financial health of the companies we are investing in.

2. **Risk Management:** Never put all your eggs in one basket. Diversify our portfolio across different asset classes and sectors to mitigate risk. Also, always set a stop-loss limit to minimize potential losses.

3. **Discipline:** Stick to the trading plan. It's easy to get swayed by short-term market fluctuations, but remember, our strategy is based on long-term gains. 

4. **Emotional Control:** Trading can be a roller-coaster of emotions. It's crucial to remain calm and composed, especially during market volatility. Avoid making impulsive decisions based on fear or greed.

5. **Continuous Learning:** The financial market is continuously evolving. Stay updated with the latest news, market trends, and trading techniques. 

6. **Performance Review:** Regularly review and analyze our trading performance. This will help us understand what's working and what's not, and make necessary adjustments to our strategy.

7. **Patience:** Remember, trading is not a get-rich-quick scheme. It requires patience and persistence. Successful trading is about consistently making profitable trades over time, not about making a big win overnight.

In conclusion, a logic-based trading strategy is about making informed decisions, managing risk, maintaining discipline, controlling emotions, continuously learning, reviewing performance, and being patient. Let's keep these points in mind as we navigate the trading landscape.
