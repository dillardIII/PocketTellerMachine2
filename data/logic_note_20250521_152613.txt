2. **Fundamental Analysis:** We will analyze economic indicators, industry conditions, and company financials to assess a company's intrinsic value. If a company's intrinsic value is higher than its market value, it might be a good investment opportunity.

3. **Risk Management:** We will never risk more than a small percentage of our trading capital on a single trade. This will protect us from significant losses. 4. **Emotional Control:** Trading can be emotionally challenging. We will maintain discipline and avoid making impulsive decisions based on fear or greed. We will stick to our trading plan and strategy, regardless of short-term market fluctuations.

5. **Continuous Learning:** The market is dynamic and constantly changing. We will keep ourselves updated with the latest market news, trends, and strategies. We will also review our trades regularly to learn from our successes and mistakes.

6. **Diversification:** We will diversify our portfolio by investing in different asset classes and sectors. This will spread the risk and increase the potential for returns.
