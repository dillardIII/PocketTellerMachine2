6. **Performance Review**: Regularly reviewing our trading performance is crucial. This will help us identify what's working and what's not, allowing us to make necessary adjustments to our strategy.

7. **Use of Technology**: We should leverage technology to our advantage. Using trading software can help us execute trades more efficiently and accurately. It can also help us analyze market trends and make more informed decisions.
